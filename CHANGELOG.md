## 1.0.0

- Initial release.
- Bluetooth LE connection to thermal printers via `flutter_blue_plus`.
- ESC/POS receipt generation for 3-inch and 4-inch paper.
- Configurable receipt layout: logo, GST/TRN, customer fields, decimals.
- Optional logo image (monogram) support.
- Chunked write for Bluetooth MTU limits.
- Reconnect to saved printer by MAC address.
